About us

One of the prime concerns for international students coming to the US for education is the selection of Universities. Due to lack of information and resources, the filtering process often gets biased. Studying abroad comes with a lot of challenges and risks which involve huge investments in finance and time. This uncertainty needs to be addressed with a statistical model which can help students to narrow down on a set of Universities from a broader spectrum.
